About

The person
behind the work

I'm Anson Benny— a visual designer who believes design is storytelling. Every pixel, typeface, and colour should have a reason.

I led creative operations for Jesus Youth, IEEE and TEDxAJCE simultaneously for two years — managing everything from event identity systems to last-minute social media posts. That pressure taught me how to think fast, maintain quality, and collaborate without losing the thread of the design.

My internship at Riverrises Technologies added real client experience — working under live briefs, tight deadlines, and brand consistency requirements. I know how the handoff works, and I've lived the full creative pipeline from brief to published asset.

Outside design, my time as a Jesus Youth full-timer sharpened how I work with people — with patience, purpose, and genuine care for the outcome. Not just delivering, but delivering well.
